Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally
and internatioanlly renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain our audience! As one of our members told us:

"Each year I give a ticket package to our family members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge...and that is priceless."

Speaker of the Month

Fossil Threads in the web of life

February

Scott Sampson

What's 75 million years old and brand spanking new? A teenage Utahceratops! Come to the Saroyan, armed with your best dinosaur roar, when Scott Sampson, Researcher Curator at Utah Museum of Natural History, steps to the podium. Sampson's research has focused on the ecology and evolution of late Cretaceous dinosaurs and he has conducted fieldwork in a number of countries in Africa. Read more. Or meet us there !
